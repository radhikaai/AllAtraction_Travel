Q. Should I submit any document for the application of an India visa?
A. You will be asked to submit the following for the prompt approval of your visa.
- Duly filled out India visa application form
- Passport with a validity of at least six months
- Recent color passport-size photo taken against white background
- Visa fee as specified by the embassy

Q. Do kids require a visa to visit India?
A. Yes, kids of all age groups will need a valid visa to enter India.

Q. Once my visa application is submitted, how many days will it take for the visa processing?
A. The processing time of an India visa is two to three working days.

Q. What is the validity of an Indian E-Visa?
A. An Indian E-Visa is valid for fully 30 days.

Q. Can I extend my India visa?
A. No; it is not possible to extend an India visa.

Q. Is visa fee refundable if my visa application is rejected?
A. No matter you cancel your visa or it is rejected, an India e-Visa is non-refundable once it is submitted to the Embassy.
