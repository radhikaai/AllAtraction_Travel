Activity Overview

Make your Dubai vacation as adventurous and fun-filled as possible with our diverse Dubai Parks and Resorts™ passes! Select our Single Day Passes or Multi-Day Pass; each of them offers a variety of flexible options (See our ‘Inclusions’ section for more details). Credited to be the first Integrated Resort in the region, Dubai Parks and Resorts™ has three theme parks, one water park, a Polynesian themed family resort, and a dedicated retail and dining section, in its colossal 16 million square feet site. Riverland, with a host of leisure, dining and retail choices, serves as the gateway to this resort. With four themed areas including Boardwalk, French Village, the Peninsula and India Gate, it lets visitors to step back in time and also revel in several festivals as well as concerts. MOTIONGATE™ Dubai is one of the three theme parks that make up the key attractions of this resort. Filled with most advanced rides, special kids’ rides, and motion simulators – all inspired by Hollywood movies, this theme park will take your love for cinemas, adventure, and fantasies to a whole new level. Studio Central, Smurfs Village, Dream Works, Sony Pictures, and Lions gate are its main sections. Bollywood movie lovers and those who are fascinated about Indian culture and traditions will enjoy exploring the resort’s theme park, namely, BOLLYWOOD PARKS™ Dubai. What makes this park unique is its perfect blend of rides and attractions based on popular Bollywood classics / characters, enriched with an incredible dose of adventure, fun, romance and music. One of its major attractions is Rajmahal – an 850-seater Broadway Style Theater, showcasing daily Bollywood shows in association with Wizcraft International Entertainment. Another of the theme parks is LEGOLAND® Dubai which offers 40 plus LEGO® themed rides and shows exclusively for families visiting with kids aged between 2 and 12. There are six themed sections including Factory, LEGO® City, Kingdoms, Adventure, Imagination, and Miniland. Adjacent to the park is LEGOLAND® Water Park with over 20 unique water slides. Precisely, Dubai Parks and Resorts™ is an exciting entertainment attraction promising something to cater to every preference all day long.
